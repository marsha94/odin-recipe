Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 11⁄2 lbs ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1⁄2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 11⁄2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1⁄2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1⁄4 tsp black pepper
  • 1⁄4 cup fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil the Lasagna Noodles:
    • In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente (firm to the bite).
    • Drain the noodles and set them aside, separating them to prevent sticking.

  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage until fully cooked and no longer pink.
    • Add the diced onions and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions become translucent.
    • Stir in the tomato sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
    • Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Remove it from heat.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and the egg. Mix until well blended.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a small amount of the meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread a portion of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.
    • Add more meat sauce on top of the cheese.
    • Repeat these layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil to prevent excessive browning.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the top is bubbly and lightly browned.

  6. Let it Rest:
    • Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes. This helps the lasagna set and makes it easier to slice.

  7. Garnish and Serve:
    • If desired, garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
    • Slice and serve your delicious homemade lasagna!
